Start by removing the loose dirt and debris from the hidden parts of your couth and make sure you remove the couch cushions to ensure dirt is removed from every corner.</p> <h3>Wipe it Down</h3> <p>Of course, wiping may be not do a great ideal to clean your couch, but it is essential for the metal or wooden parts of your couch.
